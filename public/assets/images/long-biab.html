BIAB Gel Nails

BIAB or Builder in a Bottle is a gel-like nail polish applied as an overlay on natural nails or short extensions. It works like a gel but it dries strong and durable, meaning you're less likely to chip between sessions.

  • Includes 2 in 1 Primer and Base - no need for extra products

  • Long-lasting formulation that can last up to 4+ weeks - no chipping or lifting

  • Designed to keep natural nails strong, whilst promoting long-lasting nail health

  • Self - levelling

  • Can be infilled or soaked off

  • Can be left as this beautiful shade or finished with a gel colour on top

  • 100% Gel system

  • LED and UV curable
